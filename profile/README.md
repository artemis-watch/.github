<h1 align="center">Artemis Watch</h1>

Artemis Watch is a non-profit that provides a collection of software designed for identifying victims and perpetrators of CSAM and online abuse. The tools are free and open source, and are designed to be used by law enforcement, NGOs, and other organizations or researchers working to combat and prevent online abuse.
